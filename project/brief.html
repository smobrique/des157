  • Topic Summary: This project involves bringing awareness to cultures that lack representation, or are constantly misrepresented - primarily in the Southeast Asian region.
  • Target Audience Description: As mentioned above, the project is primarily targeted towards Southeast Asian communities.
  • Project Goals:
    • Educate and inform others of cultures that exists outside of Eastern Asia.
    • Emphasize the importance of unity within marginalized communities.
    • Raise awareness towards those in the community that are constantly oppressed.
  • Overall look & feel:
    • I want the project to be a space of storytelling from various perspectives. I think I'm going to incorporate videos into my project to display for a desktop viewport. So far, my overall look is going to involve a wide range of colors, because Southeast Asian cultures aren't just black and white - they're very vibrant. I want to be able to embody their vibrant attitudes through my color palettes. I haven't thought about how everything's going to be set up quite yet, but I would imagine everything to be laid out in a very cohesive manner in a way that's easy for peopel to navigate through.
